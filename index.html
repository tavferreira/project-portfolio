My Thoughts

JAN 2020

Changes

It was eye-opening to see how Redux helps to simplify so many situations by having a centralized approach (with global states) and preventing passing a lot of code/props around. A lot of new concepts were introduced again these past weeks, but it feels great to realize week after week that most of them are already understood without even noticing it. No slice, reducer or action can scare me anymore. Even thunks are great friends now. >>

DEC 2019

Focused.

I've realized that the problem was never on the concepts being too hard to understand but to dedicate time to them. Technigo's bootcamp made me realize that. Week after week, even if the subject changes, you keep practicing what you learned previously and just like the body, the mind will also get better with exercise. And your self-esteem will also improve, I can assure you. >>
